Naruto

This is the best anime I have ever watched. Everything in the story-line is on point, kick ass action and emotional scenes that can make any tough guy cry. This is a show that can be enjoyed by people of all age groups. And this is coming from a hardcore anime fan. The emotional factor here is spot on and will let you take on life with a different perspective. If I were to describe it in one sentence, I would say "It's LIFE CHANGING".
~Adwait Dandekar

Oushitsu Kyoushi Heine

The show is entertaining all the way to the end. With moments to laugh about. But all lessons have a meaning behind it, and it all builds up to the last chapter, which will make sense of all the 11 episodes. Brilliant! Ps. I freaking cried towards the end of episode 12!
~teaskittles

The story is still really simple. It doesn’t have a complicated storyline and there’s not even a plot. Though that is the case, it’s still not boring at all.
